## Why "Entity is Malicious" (Not "Compromised Site")

The terminology is confusing, but here's what it means:

- **"Compromised Site"** = Your website has malware/hacking
- **"Entity is Malicious"** = Your **business model/category** is flagged as high-risk or prohibited

Google is saying your **business entity** (fantasy sports platform) falls into a restricted category, NOT that your site is hacked.
